Important Notice: Prarambh Group ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, engage with our marketing campaigns, or interact with our services.

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Fill out contact forms on our website

  • Schedule consultations or property viewings
  • Subscribe to our newsletters or updates
  • Interact with our social media pages
  • Contact us via email, phone, or other communication channels

This information may include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Property preferences and requirements
  • Budget range
  • Any other information you choose to provide

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website addresses
  • Click patterns and navigation paths
  • Device identifiers

1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and gather information about visitors and visits to our website. This includes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to track visitors across websites for advertising purposes
  • Social Media Cookies: Enable social media features and track social media interactions

We use the collected information for various purposes, including:

2.1 To Provide and Improve Our Services

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Process your requests for property information and brochures
  • Schedule property viewings and consultations

  • Improve our website, services, and customer experience
  • Conduct market research and analysis

2.2 For Marketing and Advertising

  • Send promotional emails and newsletters (with your consent)
  • Display targeted advertisements on social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, etc.)
  • Create custom and lookalike audiences for advertising campaigns
  • Retarget website visitors with relevant ads
  • Measure and analyze the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
  • Send SMS marketing messages (with your explicit consent as per TRAI regulations)

2.3 For Legal and Compliance Purposes

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Protect against fraud, spam, and abuse
  • Enforce our Terms and Conditions
  • Respond to legal requests and prevent harm

3. Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Advertising

Important Information About Meta Advertising

We use Meta's advertising tools and pixels to:

  • Track conversions and measure ad performance
  • Create custom audiences from website visitors
  • Build lookalike audiences to reach potential customers
  • Display targeted ads on Facebook and Instagram
  • Retarget users who have interacted with our content

When you interact with our Meta ads or visit our website from a Meta platform, Meta may collect information about your activity. This is governed by Meta's own privacy policies, which you can review at: facebook.com/privacy/policy

3.1 Meta Pixel and Conversions API

We have installed the Meta Pixel on our website to:

  • Track page views, form submissions, and brochure downloads
  • Measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns
  • Optimize ad delivery to reach people most likely to be interested in our properties
  • Create remarketing campaigns for website visitors

3.2 Custom Audiences

We may upload hashed email addresses and phone numbers to Meta to create Custom Audiences for advertising purposes. This data is encrypted and matched with Meta users' information to display relevant ads.

3.3 Your Rights Regarding Meta Advertising

You can control how your data is used for advertising by:

  • Adjusting your ad preferences on Facebook and Instagram
  • Opting out of interest-based advertising
  • Using browser settings to block cookies
  • Contacting us to request removal from our marketing lists

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy

Typically, we retain:
